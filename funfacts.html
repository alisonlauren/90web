Will

Will is a street-smart and laid-back West Philadelphia teenager.
After Will gets into a fight his mother sends him to live with his
wealthy aunt and uncle (Vivian and Phillip Banks) in Bel-Air

Carlton

Carlton is very unlucky when it comes to women. They often find him
unimpressive, arrogant, nerdy and obnoxious. At times when Carlton
does think he's found love, it's always revealed he's actually
being used in one way or another.

Uncle Phil

Even though Phillip tends to lose his temper fairly quickly, he means
well deep down, and he loves and cares deeply about his family.
Philip also loves food and had a pet pig when he remained young

Aunt Vivian

She is a no-nonsense, vocally talented, forthright, aspiring, understanding,
nurturing, trusting, vivacious, independent, valiant,
industrious, astute, nice and career-minded woman who plays a part in
the show equal to her husband Philip

Ashley

Ashley enjoys the time she spends with her mother, but is arguably the closest
with her cousin, Will, who is a huge inspiration to her.
Ashley looked up to Will as a big brother and spent much of her
time following him in her younger days.

Hilary

Sometimes very dim-witted and extremely self-centered, who can come off as an
arrogant airhead; she looks down on Will's upbringing and is
obsessed with shopping and money. However, despite her apparent
shallow personality, she has a strong sense of integrity
